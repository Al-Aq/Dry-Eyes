Getting Started

To get the most out of the Screen Break Reminder:

  • Add to Dock: Drag this website’s URL to your Dock for easy access.
  • Open at Login: Set your browser to open at login via System Settings > Users & Groups > Login Items.
  • Allow Notifications: Enable browser notifications to receive break reminders.
